Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

The short version

We collect your name and phone number to run the competition. We don't sell your data. You can opt out of messages anytime by replying STOP.

What we collect

When you check in at a Bar Rank participating bar we collect:

  • Your name or nickname
  • Your mobile phone number
  • Your check-in activity, points, and gameplay history at that bar
  • The date and time of your check-ins

We do not collect payment information, email addresses, location data, or any information beyond what is listed above.

How we use it

  • To track your points and rank across visits at the bar
  • To display your name or nickname on the in-bar leaderboard
  • To send you SMS messages from the bar on behalf of the bar via Bar Rank (if you have consented)
  • To generate anonymous aggregate statistics for bar owners (e.g. total check-ins, busiest nights)

Who sees your data

The bar owner and their staff can see your name, phone number, total points, and check-in history at their bar. They cannot see your activity at other bars.

Bar Rank staff can access your data for support and platform integrity purposes.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party for marketing purposes.

SMS messages

If you have consented to SMS messages at check-in, you may receive occasional messages from the bar you visited via Bar Rank. These may include competition updates, special offers, and event announcements.

To opt out of SMS messages reply STOP to any message at any time. After opting out you will not receive further messages from that bar.

Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies by bar.

Data retention

Your data is retained as long as the bar you checked into is active on the Bar Rank platform. If a bar closes or leaves the platform, your data associated with that bar may be deleted.

You can request deletion of your account by contacting us at hello@barrank.app.

Security

Your data is stored in a secure cloud database. We use industry-standard practices to protect your information. No system is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children

Bar Rank is intended for use in licensed bars and is not directed at anyone under the age of 21. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via SMS where possible.

Contact

Questions about your privacy? Contact us at hello@barrank.app
