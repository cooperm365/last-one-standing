Bar Rank — User Experience Overview Step 1 — Scan a QR code at any game table Walk up to any game at the bar — darts, pool, trivia, bingo, blackjack, live music. There's a gold card on the table with a QR code. Open your phone camera and point it at the code. No app download required. Step 2 — Enter your name and number The check-in page loads instantly. First-timers enter their name and mobile number. Returning players see their number pre-filled — just tap Check In. Your mobile number is your identity — no password, no account creation, no email required. Step 3 — Tutorial for new users First time using Bar Rank? A 7-slide visual tutorial walks you through exactly how everything works — where to find QR codes, how points are earned, what the timer means, how the leaderboard works, and what The Lounge is. Takes about 60 seconds to swipe through. Never shown again after your first visit. Step 4 — 15-minute timer starts After checking in you see a gold countdown timer. Stay at the game and play for 15 minutes to lock in your points. The timer runs server-side — if you get a call, switch apps, or close the browser, your timer keeps running. Come back to the same link and it resumes exactly where it left off. Step 5 — Points confirmed When the timer hits zero, confetti fires, a victory sound plays, and your points are confirmed with a slot-machine style counter animation. Points appear on the live leaderboard immediately. Step 6 — Scan another game You get up to 3 scans per visit on an 8-hour window. Head to a different game, scan its QR code, and the whole process repeats. Each game has its own QR — you can't scan the same game twice in one visit. Step 7 — Check your score Open your My Score page any time to see your total points, your current rank, how many scans you've used tonight, and your full point history. The page shows if you moved up or down the leaderboard since your last visit with a notification banner. Step 8 — Race to 200 points The leaderboard is displayed live on a TV in the bar. First player to reach 200 points wins the prize — currently a $50 gift card. A gold progress bar shows how close the leader is. Every player's progress toward 200 is visible. When someone wins, a winner announcement takes over the screen. Step 9 — Earn bonus points for winning On top of check-in points, staff award bonus points for wins and top performances — win a darts game, beat someone at pool, get a bingo. These get added manually and show up in your history instantly. Step 10 — The Lounge Between games, tap The Lounge from your score page. Three tabs: 🎲 Risk It — Set a wager using a slider, answer a bar trivia question. Get it right and your wager doubles. Get it wrong and you lose the points. Real stakes, real consequences. 📸 Rep Rank — Opens your camera with a gold frame overlay showing your name and rank. Take a photo, save it, and post it. Your rank is on the image. 🍺 Order Up — Pick your vibe from six options and get a drink recommendation. Two of the options point you to the bar's Shot of the Week and Drink of the Month. What makes it different: No app download — works in any browser No account creation — just a name and phone number Completely self-serve — no staff involvement required Works on any phone, any browser Points are tracked permanently — every visit builds toward the prize The leaderboard on the TV sells itself to anyone who walks in
