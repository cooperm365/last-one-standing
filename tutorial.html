WELCOME
Play games.
Earn points.
Win prizes.
Bar Rank turns your night out into a competition. Every game you play earns you points on the live leaderboard.
STEP 1 OF 4
Find the QR code
Every game has a QR code card on the table — darts, pool, trivia, bingo. Look for the gold card.
📍Each game has its own QR code. Scan as many different games as you want tonight — up to 3 per visit.
STEP 2 OF 4
Scan with your camera
Open your phone camera and point it at the QR code. It reads it automatically — no app download needed.
💡Already played before? Your number is saved — just tap check in and you're done.
STEP 3 OF 4
Enter your details
Type your name and mobile number. We use your number to track your points — no password, no account, no hassle.
🔒Your number is only used for Bar Rank. We don't send spam — ever.
STEP 4 OF 4
Stay & play for 15 mins
A 15-minute timer starts. Stay at the game and play. When it hits zero, your points are confirmed automatically.
📱Need to step away? No problem — come back to this link and your timer picks up where it left off.
THE GOAL
Race to 200 points
First player to 200 points wins the prize. Your name is on the TV leaderboard for everyone to see. Come back every visit to climb.
🏆Win bonus points for beating opponents in skill games. Staff award these — play hard!
BONUS
Hang out in The Lounge
Between games, open The Lounge from your score page. Answer bar trivia to earn bonus points, take a rank selfie to share, or get a drink recommendation based on your vibe.
🎲Get a trivia question right and earn +5 bonus points. No penalty for wrong answers!
